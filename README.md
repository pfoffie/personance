# personance

Personance is a gentle reminder system for the people, things, and activities you care about.

## What it is

Personance helps you stay connected to what matters in everyday life. Instead of acting like a strict task manager, it works as a soft companion that reminds you with intention and empathy.

## Core idea

- Keep relationships alive through thoughtful follow-ups.
- Remember meaningful activities before they fade into the background.
- Track small personal commitments without pressure.
- Build continuity in your life with calm, human-centered reminders.

## Why it exists

Modern tools are often built for urgency and productivity. Personance is built for care, memory, and consistency. It supports the emotional side of organization, not just the functional side.
